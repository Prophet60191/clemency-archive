# Welcome to the Clemency Archive Project!

This document is a guided tour to help you understand our project. Whether you have five minutes or an hour, this guide will point you to the most relevant information.

---

## 1. The Big Picture: The "Why" (5-10 Minute Read)

If you're new to the project and want to understand our mission, goals, and the problem we're solving, start here.

*   **For the Quickest Overview:** Begin with our main [**`README.md`**](./index.md). It provides a concise summary of the project's purpose and a map of the repository.

*   **For a Deeper Dive:** Read the [**`Clemency_Archive_Proposal.md`**](./Clemency_Archive_Proposal.md). This is the core document that outlines the project's rationale, objectives, and expected impact in detail.

---

## 2. The Operational Plan: The "How" (For Collaborators & Volunteers)

If you're interested in how we're actually doing the work—our timeline, data structure, and processes—this section is for you. This is essential reading for anyone considering a volunteer or contributor role.

*   **The Project Roadmap:** The [**`Master_Project_Plan.md`**](./0_Project_Management/project_binder/Master_Project_Plan.md) provides a high-level timeline of all project phases, from setup and recruitment to the final launch of the public platform.

*   **The Blueprint for Our Data:** The [**`Data_Schema_Dictionary.md`**](./0_Project_Management/project_binder/1_Data_Blueprint/Data_Schema_Dictionary.md) is the technical constitution for our project. It defines every single data field we are collecting, ensuring our data is consistent and reliable.

*   **The Data Entry Rulebook:** The [**`Data_Entry_Guide.md`**](./0_Project_Management/project_binder/1_Data_Blueprint/Data_Entry_Guide.md) is the primary training manual for our data transcribers. It provides step-by-step instructions for how to interpret the archival ledgers and enter the data accurately.

## 3. The Contributor's Path: "What You Can Do"

Ready to get involved? This section provides clear next steps based on your interests and skills.

*   **For Prospective Volunteers:** If you are interested in helping us with the crucial work of data transcription and verification, please start by reading the [**`Volunteer_Role_Descriptions.md`**](./0_Project_Management/project_binder/2_Volunteer_Management/Volunteer_Role_Descriptions.md) to see what roles are available and what skills are needed.

*   **For Developers & Technologists:** If you are interested in contributing to the custom web application and database, the [**`Technical_Requirements_Document.md`**](./0_Project_Management/project_binder/3_Technical_Tools/Technical_Requirements_Document.md) outlines the high-level technical specifications and goals for the platform.
