# Volunteer Role Descriptions

**Purpose:** To recruit effectively and set clear expectations for each volunteer role.

---

## Role: Data Transcriber

*   **Responsibilities:**
    *   Transcribe data from scanned historical documents into our data entry tool, following the `Data_Entry_Guide.md` precisely.
    *   You may be asked to perform either the first or the second transcription of a record as part of our double-entry system.
    *   Flag records with illegible handwriting or unclear information for review using the `needs_review` flag.
*   **Skills Needed:**
    *   High attention to detail and patience.
    *   Comfortable working with digital tools and historical documents.
    *   Ability to read historical handwriting is a plus, but not required. Our guides provide help!
*   **Time Commitment:** Flexible, but aiming for 3-5 hours per week is ideal.

---

## Role: Team Lead

*   **Responsibilities:**
    *   Manage and support a small team of approximately 5-10 Data Transcribers.
    *   Assign record batches to volunteers according to the `Verification_Workflow.md`.
    *   **Perform the critical verification step:** Compare the two independent transcriptions (Entry A and Entry B) for each record.
    *   Investigate and resolve any data discrepancies by referring to the original source document.
    *   Serve as the primary point of contact for your team's questions and provide encouraging feedback.
    *   Report team progress, challenges, and data quality metrics to the Project Leader.
*   **Skills Needed:**
    *   Proven experience as a high-quality and reliable Data Transcriber on this project.
    *   Exceptional attention to detail.
    *   Strong leadership, mentorship, and communication skills.
    *   Ability to motivate and support a volunteer team.
*   **Time Commitment:** Estimated 5-7 hours per week.
